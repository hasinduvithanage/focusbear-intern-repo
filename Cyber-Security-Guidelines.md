What are common cyber security threats in a remote work environment?
- Phishing emails
- Unsecure personal devices
- Weak passwords

What are best practices for keeping your devices and accounts secure?
- By making sure that the latest versions of software are present.
- By making sure that my devices auto lock
- By using strong passwords

Why is it important to lock your computer when away from your desk?
- This will prevent unauthorized physical access.
- Unauthorized users will have access to the company's entire codebase if they get access to the device.

How should you handle phishing attempts and suspicious links?
- Check the sender's email, not just the display name.
- Double check the link before clicking on it.
- Report a phishing email if I come acroos it, so that other can be warned.

What makes a strong password, and why should you use a password manager?
- A strong password should be long, more than 8 characters long
- It should be unique
- It should contain uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers and symbols.

Why is two-factor authentication (2FA) important, and when should you enable it?
- This is a second layer of protection since if your password stolen, a hacker still will not be able to get in the device wothout the temporary code.
- M2FA should be enabled always.
