What are the key roles in an Agile team, and what does each role do?
Developers
  In this role, the persons are responsible for writing the code and making sure that the product is created per the requirements. They are responsible for the technical execution including soltuion design.
  
QA (Quality Assurance)
  In this role, they work alongside the developers to carry out testing, finding bugs and making sure that the product is upto the standard as expected.
  
Product Manager (PM)
  The people in this role define the reason for the product, manage the product's vision, prioritize the backlog and make sure that the team is building something that actually solves the problem that the consumer is having.

Agile Project Manager (Agile PM)
  The people in this role makes sure that the team's work is going smoothly by removing any blockers that the team has, and also fascilates the communications between the team members. They help keep the team on task.
  
Other supporting roles (e.g., stakeholders, UX, customer support)
  Stakeholders: They help provide the feedback and helps with the business side of the company.
  Customer support: They help the customers when they have questions or problems with the product or service. They can also feed the customer complaints back to the project manager so that they can use that information to improve the product.
  UX designer: They help design the overall user experience of the software. Their goal is to improve usability.
